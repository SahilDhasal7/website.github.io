biography

Dr. Rahul Patil is a highly skilled and accomplished physician, specializing in Medicine, and the proud founder of Vihaan Hospital. With a deep-rooted passion for healthcare and a commitment to delivering excellence, Dr. Patil has dedicated his career to providing comprehensive and compassionate medical care to patients.

After completing his medical education, Dr. Rahul Patil pursued his specialization in Medicine, earning the prestigious qualification of Diplomate of National Board (DNB). His extensive training and expertise allow him to diagnose and manage a wide range of medical conditions with precision and care.

With a relentless pursuit of medical advancements and a strong focus on patient well-being, Dr. Rahul Patil and his team at Vihaan Hospital continue to strive for excellence in providing comprehensive medical services. Their collective goal is to make a positive impact on the lives of patients and contribute to the overall well-being of the community they serve.
